Hobbies & Creative Pursuits

Family & Home

Spending quality time with my family is at the heart of everything I do. Whether it’s cooking together, exploring new recipes, or enjoying simple everyday moments, these times are deeply meaningful and grounding. Home is where my creativity and peace come together.

Painting

Painting helps me express emotions and ideas beyond words. I love experimenting with colors and textures — creating scenes that reflect my thoughts or moods. My art often blends natural elements with abstract concepts, offering both serenity and freedom in expression.

Sewing

I find immense joy in sewing, transforming fabric into something beautiful and useful. It’s both a creative outlet and a relaxing escape, allowing me to focus, plan, and craft something entirely from imagination. Whether it’s a small piece or a detailed outfit, every project tells a personal story.
